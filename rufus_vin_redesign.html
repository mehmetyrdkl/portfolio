Rufus Vin Redesign

For the Rufus Vin Redesign, me and my group had an assignment of finding a company in need of a website/brand redesign. We found this small company that imports wines from smaller wineries across Europe, specializing in cold climate wines, Rufus Vin.

From the inital talks with the owner of the company we understood that they are in need of a brand new website, with the functionality of a webstore as their current solution is only informative, not allowing the customers to browse and show for their products online.

For their store page, we created a database with curated products to show the client our capabilities as multimedia designers. We took photos of each individual products and adjusted them for lightning and color corrected them.

My role in this assignent was to work as a "project manager" for the team, as well as building and working on the web solution, focusing on the content pages, as well as using a database made in WordPress specifically for the news page, fetching data from that said database, in order to enable our client to add new posts without having to deal with all unnecessary trouble of coding.
